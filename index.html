About Me

My first time as a child diving into the marvelous world structured by video games left an indelible impression on me. Since then, I never cease to be overwhelmed by the charm of games, which allows me to play different roles, undertake exciting adventures and make heroic deeds in new realities. Hence, becoming a great video game developer like Hideo Kojima has become my career ambition. I aspire to develop an award-winning game that has a positive and lasting impact on its players, especially teenagers who crave recognition and guidance.

My motto is to be a Life-long Learner, so I constantly learn about topics related to Computer Science and Game Development via MOOC platforms. In my spare time, I have taken five internships in game engineering or related roles. I am also interested in Game Design. I have read the book A Little History of World, The Story of Art, The Design of Everyday Things, The Laws of Simplicity, A Theory of Fun, Introduction to Game Design, Prototyping and Development, A Simple Introduction to Game Mechanics, Game Engine Architecture, Artificial Intelligence and Games, Chris Crawford on Interactive Storytelling, Blood Sweat and Pixels, Ask Iwata, The Creative Gene, Reality is Broken and am reading The Art of Game Design. I want to be a Game Engineer, then Technical Designer, and Creative Director in the future.
